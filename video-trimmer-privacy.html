Privacy Policy

Video Trimmer (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

Information We Collect

Video Trimmer does not collect or store any personal information from your device. The app only requests permissions needed to select, trim, and save videos locally on your device. All processing is done on your device, and no media is uploaded to any server.

Permissions Used

  • Storage: To read and save trimmed video files.
  • Camera / Microphone (optional): Only if you use in-app video recording features.

Children’s Privacy

Video Trimmer is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
